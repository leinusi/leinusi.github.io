Publications & Research (Selected)

Cascaded ViT with Monte Carlo Dropout for Sequence-to-Sequence Ensemble Forecasting

S. Liang, et al. — Status: Under review (2024)
Abstract: We introduce a lightweight sequence-to-sequence ensemble forecasting framework that combines an anchor-based low-dimensional signal extraction (LightGBM) with a 12-layer encoder Vision Transformer enhanced by ARM convolution to mitigate checkerboard artifacts. Monte Carlo Dropout is used to produce ensemble members within a single inference pass, delivering a balance of predictive accuracy and computational efficiency suitable for resource-constrained deployment.
Key results: 34.3% reduction in ensemble spread; 8.1% improvement in monthly mean MSE; ~50% faster inference; ~56% lower memory usage relative to baseline ensemble models.
Paper (PDF) Code (open)

TSCC: Two-Stage Cascaded Self-Supervised Clustering for Radar Echo Classification

S. Liang, et al. — Submitted to Journal of Geophysical Research (2024)
Abstract: We propose a two-stage cascaded clustering pipeline for radar echo classification. In stage one we construct category prototypes from high-quality center samples using self-supervised representation learning; in stage two, those prototypes initialize clustering on lower-quality samples. We incorporate spatial continuity constraints (neighborhood voting, altitude-level consistency, connected-region optimization) to improve spatial coherence and robustness when labels are scarce.
Evaluation: Improvements of 20–35% on Silhouette/DB/CH metrics; ~15% increase in NMI; ~12% increase in ARI under limited-label settings; ~25% enhancement in THI spatial coherence.
Manuscript Data & code (planned)

Multi-Agent Framework for Automated Feature Engineering in Financial Markets

S. Liang, et al. — Technical report (2024)
Abstract: We develop an MCTS-based multi-agent search framework (PUCT priors) for automated factor discovery in financial markets. The framework integrates market-state-aware reward shaping and gating mechanisms to improve out-of-sample stability, and provides a reusable factor template library with automated evaluation and backtesting pipelines.
Highlights: Automated candidate generation with Pareto-aware selection; improved OOS robustness in backtests; modular pipeline for reproducible feature discovery and evaluation.
